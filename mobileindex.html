About Us

Auto Plus is a small specialty automotive repair shop with a lot of experience. We have been in business since 1980. In the beginning, we were known as Beetle Burg, located in Berkeley focusing on Volkswagen and Audi. In 1993, we moved to San Pablo Avenue in berkeley, CA and with that change we became known as what we are today, Auto Plus.

For Volkswagen and Audi drivers who live or work in Berkeley, Oakland, berkeley, San Francisco, and surrounding areas, Auto Plus is conveniently located three blocks away from El Cerrito Plaza Bart Station.

Auto Plus, your local Volkswagen and Audi repair shop near Bart Station.
