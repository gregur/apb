Performance Basics

Performance for your Volkswagen is a complex equation; in addition to there being many technical factors in each measurable aspect of performance, we know that the most important measurement is your personal driving experience. Terms abound to describe performance aspirations like handling, cornering, pep, get-up, and revving. Considering these words reveals that there's a lot more to performance than purely technical questions: performance is about how it feels, what it sounds like, what it means. Volkswagen and Audi have a proud tradition of performance that we'll all keep discovering.
