Let's Hang Out!

The Bay Area is a place of rich culture and abundant events. We keep tabs on what people are doing in our community in order to never be out of the loop with our customers. We maintain a Twitter feed that points to some of the best events in the neighborhood and insider tips that you won't find anywhere else. We aim to stay in touch with our customers, more than just any mechanic shop because we know that East Bay people like to be friendly and neighborly, that our role is to be a part of the community and to give back. Whether you're aVolkswagen/Audi enthusiast or you're just looking for new connections in your community, our customers are like-minded folks who have hundreds of passions and interests to share, and we aim to support them with coordination.

Fun Activities on Twitter

We post the best events and attractions in the East Bay to our Twitter feed. Look for arts and culture, music, film, science events, and sports. We post classic car exhibitions, parades, park performances and trivia nights. And we make sure to locate the most diverse and appealing events for families, students, professors, and professionals in the east bay. If you're a Cal affiliate or a Cal sports fan, we have information to support your interests with activities, events, and special offers. Take your Cal pride to Auto Plus Berkeley and sign up for cool updates and offers through Twitter, Facebook, and Foursquare!

Learn Something New About Your Car

Our customers love to know the nitty-gritty details of their highly-engineeredVolkswagen and Audi cars. Although they range from scientists to musicians to parents and grandparents, our customers appreciate learning clever tips to drive safer and better in the East Bay. Keep tabs on our social media to learn fun new things about your car every week!
